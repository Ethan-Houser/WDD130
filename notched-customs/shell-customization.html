Shell Customization

Don't want to buy a whole new controller, but you want your controller to have a new look? No worries, we've got you covered. Send in your controller and we can give it a custom paint job. This includes hydro dipping, hand painting, and spray painting. We can even go as far as giving the controller a matte or glossy look and feel. Sometimes having a matte paintjob can give the controller a “grippier” feel so that it better sits in your hands while you are playing.
