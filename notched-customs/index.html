High Quality Custom Controllers at your Fingertips

We provide controllers with unique custom designs and button feel. We design and paint the shell of the controller (or the outside plastic) to fit your unique style. The colors of the buttons can also be customized to fit the theme of the rest of the controller. The controllers can be hydro dipped, hand painted, or spray painted. This gives you countless options in color and design. We also provide the option to have your buttons sanded. This is to help your fingers glide over the buttons to make it easier to make fast inputs without accidentally hitting the wrong button. Typically, only the A button is sanded, but other buttons can be sanded according to your needs. We can also change how the bumpers on the controller feel. By using different strengths of springs, we can make the bumpers have a lighter, more sensitive touch. This is great for people looking to have an edge on the competition.
